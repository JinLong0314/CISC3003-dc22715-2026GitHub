Reflection & Future Directions

Looking Back

My journey at the University of Macau has been transformative. From a curious freshman to a seasoned competitor in cybersecurity, I've grown both technically and personally. Each competition, internship, and teaching experience has contributed to shaping my professional identity and reinforcing my passion for security.

Key Insights

The most important lesson I've learned is that cybersecurity is not just about tools and techniques — it's about mindset. The ability to think like an attacker while defending like a guardian is what separates good security professionals from great ones. Continuous learning and community engagement are essential.

Path Forward

As I prepare to graduate, I am excited about the opportunities ahead. Whether in industry or academia, I plan to focus on the intersection of AI and cybersecurity. The increasing sophistication of cyber threats demands innovative approaches, and I want to be at the forefront of developing those solutions.
